About Me

I am a third year computer science student, and have been interested in the field since highschool. I started by making small games and helper programs on my TI-84 in my Physics 20-1 class using TI-Basic, and I've been hooked ever since. I have become increasingly interested in the technological leaps that the computer science field has made in both software and hardware in recent years, and am excited to start contributing to these developments. Some of my other interests include gaming, playing music, and reading. I am a passionate individual who is always eager for opportunities to further develop my knowledge and skills in these aforementioned areas.
